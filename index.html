Mass Shooting: Any single shooting incident that leaves 4 or more people injured or killed not including the shooter.

Many states have seen an increase in the number of mass shootings, as well as the number of injuries and fatalities that result from them. This map shows the number of incidents relative to each state's population size. As the reader, you can see which states have higher mass shooting rates and how mass shooting statistics for a particular state have changed over the past five years. I hope you learn that mass shooting are not a rare occurence and that they occur nearly everyday.
